How it works

This check-in is for people who want to reflect on how chemsex is showing up in their lives right now.

  1. Select the statements that fit where you're at today.

How to use this page

There are no scores and no right answers. Use it to notice what feels good, what feels difficult, and what you may want to change.

Tip
